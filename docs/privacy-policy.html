VoxRider Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

VoxRider is a mobile application that connects to a Garmin Varia radar via Bluetooth and delivers voice alerts to road cyclists. This policy explains what data the app accesses, how it is used, and what leaves your device.

1. Data We Collect

VoxRider collects no personal data. Specifically:

  • No account registration or login is required.
  • No analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry SDKs are included.
  • No usage data is transmitted to any server.
  • No advertising identifiers are accessed.

2. Bluetooth Access

VoxRider uses your device's Bluetooth radio exclusively to communicate with a paired Garmin Varia RTL515 radar unit. Bluetooth data (vehicle proximity and speed readings) is processed entirely on-device in real time and is never stored or transmitted.

On Android 12 and above the app requests BLUETOOTH_SCAN and BLUETOOTH_CONNECT permissions. On Android 11 and below, ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION is required by the operating system to perform Bluetooth scans; VoxRider does not use this permission to determine your physical location.

3. Local Storage

VoxRider stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • The Bluetooth device ID of your paired Varia radar, so the app can reconnect automatically on next launch.
  • Your in-app settings (alert verbosity, distance units).

This data never leaves your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

4. Background Operation

VoxRider runs a foreground service on Android to maintain the Bluetooth connection while your screen is locked. A persistent notification ("VoxRider active") is shown whenever the service is running, as required by Android system policy. No background data collection occurs.

5. Third-Party Services

VoxRider does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics platforms, advertising networks, or cloud services.

6. Protocol Disclaimer

VoxRider communicates with the Garmin Varia radar using a reverse-engineered Bluetooth Low Energy protocol. VoxRider is an independent project and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or supported by Garmin Ltd. Use of the app is at your own risk. The radar alert system is intended as a supplementary awareness tool only — it does not replace attentive riding or substitute for traffic law compliance.

7. Safety Disclaimer

VoxRider provides audio alerts to assist situational awareness. It does not guarantee detection of all approaching vehicles. Always ride attentively and obey all applicable traffic laws. The developers accept no liability for accidents or injuries arising from reliance on the app.

8. Children's Privacy

VoxRider is intended for adult cyclists. The app does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

Any material changes to this policy will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact
